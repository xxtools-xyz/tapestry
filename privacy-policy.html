Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal information required for functional synchronization. Specifically, we store your email address (from your registration) via Supabase to track your usage limits and membership tier.

2. Payment Processing

Payments are processed exclusively by Paddle.com (Merchant of Record). Paddle collects your billing information in accordance with their Privacy Policy. We only store the resulting User ID and Tier status in our database.

3. Direct Data Flow

Your Tapestry journal content (photos, videos, notes) is downloaded directly from Tapestry's servers to your local machine. We do not store, intercept, or see your private Tapestry data.

4. Tapestry Login

We use Tapestry's official login page embedded in the app for authentication. The app extracts a session cookie to perform the downloads. We never see or store your Tapestry password.
